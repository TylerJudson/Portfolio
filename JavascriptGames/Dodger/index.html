• Use the arrow keys to navigate the Space Ship.
  • Watch out for the falling blocks.
  • Press f to fire a projectile.
  • Press spacebar to clear the entire screen.
  • The triangles in the botttom left corner signify your health.
  • Collect falling triangles to get more lives.
  • The stars in the bottom right corner are how many clears you have left.
  • Collect falling stars to get more clears.
  • Good Luck!
