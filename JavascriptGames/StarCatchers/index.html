Star Catchers

How To Play

  • The goal is to have a higher score than your opponent at the end of the game.
  • The wasd keys control movement for red and the arrow keys control movement for blue.
  • Collect stars using your rocket to gain points.
  • Collecting a yellow star will earn you 1 point.
  • Collecting stars of the same color as your rocket will earn you 3 points!
  • Collecting stars of the opposing color will dock you 1 point!
  • Good Luck!
